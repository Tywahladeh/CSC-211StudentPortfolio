About

Genius Annotation

“yes, and?” is the lead single from Ariana Grande’s seventh studio album, eternal sunshine.

On January 1, 2024, an AG7 sign-up link appeared in the bio of Ariana Grande’s second Instagram account, @sweetener. Moreover, all previous posts were removed from the page and it was later used to promote the single with a presave link.

Ariana Grande first teased the song title on January 4, 2024, when she was spotted in NYC wearing a custom “yes, and?” sweatshirt. Celebrity photographer Miles Diggs posted two photographs of this on Instagram. This immediately caught the attention of fans as it is not the first time that she has used clothing to tease a song.

On January 7, 2024, Ariana Grande confirmed the single’s title, release date, and cover art via an Instagram post. It was confirmed by U.S. Radio Updater that the track was produced by Grande and two of her main collaborators Max Martin and ILYA. It was also confirmed the track “heavily interpolates” Madonna’s 1990 single “Vogue.”

How did the song perform on the billboard charts?
Genius Answer

“yes, and?” debuted at #1 and charted globally. The song becomes the 1,162nd No. 1 in the Hot 100’s 65-year archive.

The song marks her eighth Hot 100 #1 appearance and her fourth without a feature, following “Die for You (Remix)” with The Weeknd (2023), “Save Your Tears (Remix)" with The Weeknd (2021), “positions” (2020), “Rain on Me” with Lady Gaga (2020), “Stuck With U" with Justin Bieber (2020), “7 rings" (2019) and “thank u, next" (2018).
