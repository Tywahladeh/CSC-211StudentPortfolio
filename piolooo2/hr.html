CAREERS

Thank you for your interest in working with Rowman & Littlefield! 

We are one of the largest independent book publishers and book distributors in North America. Headquartered near Washington, D.C., the company also holds offices in Connecticut, Massachusetts, and Pennsylvania. Our passion is directed toward publishing non-fiction scholarly titles in the areas of academia, reference, professional, government, K-12 education, and associations. The  trade division of Rowman & Littlefield publishes in lifestyle, outdoor sports and recreation, and regional and local interest. Our sister distribution company, National Book Network (NBN), is located in Blue Ridge Summit, PA and provides our customers with book sales, marketing and distribution services.
