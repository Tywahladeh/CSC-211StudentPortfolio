Featured Publishers

  • Durvile & UpRoute Books

    Durvile & UpRoute Books is a small-but-mighty indie press that publishes books by authors who have the foresight to bring new knowledge and wisdom to the world. The Durvile imprint publishes the True Cases series and fascinating biographies, and the UpRoute imprint focuses on the Spirit of Nature Indigenous series, the Every River Lit series, and other brave artistic and cultural ventures.

  • Helen Exley London

    Helen Exley® London, is an independent Giftbook Publisher that was conceived by Helen Exley and her husband Richard in 1976. Helen has collected a vast body of over half a million beautiful, thoughtful, witty or wise quotations throughout her life. Their books have sold in over forty languages, including Icelandic, Welsh, Faroese, Mongolian, Albanian, Lithuanian, Japanese, Arabic and Chinese.

  • Kalaniot Books

    The mission of Kalaniot Books is to help young children and their families explore the diverse mosaic of Jewish culture and history. Their exciting picture books entertain, educate, and inspire engagement with this storied tradition. The name of their company, Kalaniot Books, comes from the bright red wild poppies or “kalaniot” that color the springtime hillsides of Israel. Like these brilliant wildflowers, their books will make Judaism a vibrant part of children’s lives.

Featured Books

  • Hooray for Craft Beer!

    Brewers Publications

    Using wit and humor and 100% illustrations, Hooray for Craft Beer! is an entertaining and informative journey through the history and world of craft beer. Readers will explore every aspect of beer from the ingredients and brewing process to glassware and how to taste beer, as they embark on a whirlwind trip around the world to discover the origin of favorite beer styles. Hooray for Craft Beer! is a simple, easy-to-read guide to learning featuring delightful illustrations.

  • Clay Succulent Garden

    C&T Publishing / Stash Books

    Lacking a green thumb? There's no need to worry when you can create your own everlasting succulents with versatile air-dry clay. Designs are included for over 25 popular succulent species, such as sedum, echeveria, and haworthia, and unique plants like cacti, air plants, and the Venus flytrap.
